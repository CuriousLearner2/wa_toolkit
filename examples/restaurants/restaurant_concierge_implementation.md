# Implementation Guide: Setting Up Your Digital Concierge
**A Step-by-Step Plan for Restaurant Owners & Managers**

Getting your WhatsApp Digital Concierge up and running is a straightforward process. You don't need to write code; you just need to provide the "rules of the house" so the AI knows how to represent your restaurant.

---

## Step 1: Prepare Your "House Rules"
Before we turn the bot on, we need to define exactly how it should behave. Please have the following ready:

*   **Your Menu:** A clear list of items, descriptions, and prices.
*   **Reservation Rules:** 
    *   Maximum table size for auto-booking (e.g., "Auto-book up to 6 people; larger groups must talk to a manager").
    *   Time slots (e.g., every 30 minutes from 5 PM to 10 PM).
    *   **Waitlist Activation:** Decide if you want the bot to offer a waitlist automatically when slots are full.
*   **Pickup Details:** Where should customers go? How should they pay? (e.g., "Pay at the counter" or "A payment link will be sent").

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your WhatsApp Identity
The bot needs a "home."
*   **The Number:** We recommend using a dedicated WhatsApp Business number. You can use your existing landline or a new mobile number.
*   **The Profile:** Add your logo, address, and hours of operation so customers see a professional storefront.

---

## Step 3: Training the Concierge (The "Prompt")
This is where we tell the AI who it is. We will set up a "personality profile" for your bot. For example:
> *"You are the concierge for 'Blue Bistro.' You are polite, efficient, and professional. Always confirm the number of guests for reservations. If a customer asks about allergies, tell them to wait for a human manager."*

---

## Step 4: The Test Drive
Before your customers see the bot, we will give you a "test link."
*   **Internal Testing:** You and your staff can text the bot to try and "break" it. Try making a reservation for a date you're closed, or ordering something off-menu.
*   **Refinement:** We will adjust the bot’s answers based on your feedback during this phase.

---

## Step 5: Going Live
Once you are happy with the bot’s performance:
*   **QR Code:** We will provide a QR code to place on your tables or front door. Customers just scan it to start chatting.
*   **Social Media:** Add a "Chat with us on WhatsApp" button to your Instagram and Facebook pages.

---

## Step 6: Daily Operations
How does your team stay in the loop?
*   **The Dashboard:** You will have a simple view where you can see all active reservations, waitlists, and incoming orders in real-time.
*   **Automatic Fill:** If you mark a reservation as "Cancelled" on your dashboard, the bot will automatically reach out to the next person on the waitlist for you.
*   **The "Human Handoff":** If a customer types "HELP" or "MANAGER," the bot will instantly send a notification to your staff's phone or tablet, allowing you to take over the conversation immediately.
